Professional Photographer

Before committing to the lens, I spent years observing the world from a different vantage point. Traveling thousands of miles across continents taught me more about light, grit, and human stories than any textbook could.

Today, based in Limerick, I translate that world-wise perspective into high-end photography. I don't just capture frames; I build narratives for those who value authenticity and hard-earned expertise.
